The Laptop Is....

A laptop computer, often abbreviated to a laptop alone, also called a laptop or laptop, is a small laptop computer with an "oyster" shape factor, usually a thin LCD screen or a LED computer screen installed inside. The top of the "clamshell" and a numeric keypad are in the inside of the bottom cover. "Clamshell" is opened to use the computer. Folded laptops are closed for transportation and are therefore suitable for mobile use. Its name comes from a "lap", also considered to be placed for use on a person's bosom. Although there was originally a distinction between notebooks and notebooks, the first one was larger and heavier than the second, and as of 2014, there was no longer a difference. Laptops are commonly used in a variety of settings, such as work, education, gaming, web browsing, personal multimedia, and the use of a home computer.

Laptop computers combine all I / O components and desktop capabilities, including the display, micro speakers, keyboard, hard drive, optical drive, pointing devices (such as the touchpad or touchpad), processor, and memory in one unit. Most modern laptops feature compact webcams and integrated microphones, while many have touch screens. Laptops can be powered either from an internal battery or from an external power source from an AC adapter. Hardware specifications, such as processor speed and memory capacity, vary widely between different types, models, and price models.







Design elements, form factor and construction can vary considerably between models based on intended use. Examples of specialized laptop models include powerful laptops for use in military construction or applications, as well as low cost laptops such as those made by One Laptop for Every Child (OLPC), which include features such as solar charging and components Semi-flexible. Found on most laptops. The laptops, which later evolved into modern laptops, were originally a small market, mostly for specialized field applications, such as military applications, accountants or mobile sales representatives. With the development of laptops to modern laptops, they have become widely used for a variety of purposes.
